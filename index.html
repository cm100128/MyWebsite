Stardew Valley is a calm game where the player(s) have to make a living by farming, fishing, mining, foraging and more, doing so while making friendships in Pelican town. It was realeased by ConcernedApe on Febuary 16th, 2016. The game initially starts off with the player(s) escaping their old office job, and they move to Pelican town in the Valley. They soon learn to love it, while growing their farm.
